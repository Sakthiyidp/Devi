Devi Interior Designer

Raised in Kumbakonam educated in Kumbakonam with a diploma in interior designing from JRVGTI. Mangesh Hande strong passion for design led him along the way to his own small successful design firm, Devi established in the 20 year's. Where with a diverse group clients, completing a multitude of projects and many private residences.

Devi is a full commercial and residential interior design firm located in Kumbakonam. It has gained recognition for its ability to infuse creativity in residential projects, commercial schemes , corporate offices and project while keeping in mind the clients requirements and parameters of the site.

Devi interiors has been working with lots of turnkey project .lots of commercial and residential project and also working modular furniture for offices .using the finest materials and superior workmanship to create work living spaces that enhance the quality of life.

In addition to customs –built furniture , Devi interior is also proud to be associated with a plethora of clients including n number of organizations to residential end user clients . The strength of the firm is its integrity , dynamic attachment of projects, commitment to quality and deadlines and warm public relations.
